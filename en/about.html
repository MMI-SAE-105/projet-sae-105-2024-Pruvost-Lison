Presentation

My name is Lison, I am a student in BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions) at the University of Belfort-Montbéliard in Montbéliard. This website is a project called "NextWear", around the subject “prehistory of the future”. My goal is therefore rather a concept. The theme of fashion allows to offer a futuristic vision with techwear and to offer original and innovative content to all the people who like fashion content.

The initial project

The "Prehistory of the Future" project aims to create a mobile-first website that explores the convergence between the past and/or our perception of the future. Through a series of articles, the site presents objects, films, books and music that illustrate how our current innovations could be interpreted by future generations. This project invites us to reflect on our impact on the future while offering an interactive and immersive experience.
