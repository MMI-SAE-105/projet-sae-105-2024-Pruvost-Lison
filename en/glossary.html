glossary

You haven't understood a word?

Techwear :

An avant-garde clothing trend that combines functionality and aesthetics, often characterized by technical materials and futuristic designs.

Intelligent fabrics :

Materials capable of reacting to their environment, such as changing color or temperature, offering thus functionality.

Futuristic fashion :

A clothing style that anticipates future trends, often inspired by science fiction and technological advances.

Retro-futurism:

A style that blends elements of the past with visions of the future, often used in fashion and design.

Clothing customization :

The process of adapting garments to the wearer's individual preferences, often facilitated by technology.

Mode durable :

The practice of creating clothing using ecological materials and ethical production methods to reduce environmental impact.

Human augmentation :

A concept in which technology is integrated into the human body, often represented in fashion by clothing with advanced functionalities.
