Music : Immersion in the future

Written on October 17, 2024
Reading time 4 min

XG, the innovative girl group, fuses music and fashion. Their track “GRL GVNG” is accompanied by a captivating futuristic clip, immersing viewers in a dystopian universe.

Unique outfits

members of the Japanese group XG

Each XG member wears a unique outfit, fusing metallic textures, holographic patterns holographic patterns and intricate geometric shapes. These garments seem to react to the environment, changing color and form and shape to the rhythm to the music.

The XG group thus demonstrates its ability to innovate not only in terms of music, but also in terms of visual presentation.

This innovative approach to costume design for music videos could well influence the future of fashion in the entertainment industry. It opens the way to new forms of artistic expression artistic expression, where technology becomes a creative tool in its own right.

With “GRL GVNG”, XG don't just follow trends: they create them. This clip marks a milestone in the evolution of musical and clothing aesthetics.
